Installation:

Place the .spritefont file inside your "engine / textures " file and RENAME to "FONT" (case sensitive).

Fonts avaliable:

* Tomb1_Tomb2_Font - TR1 / TR2 font. Colour the text in-engine manually.
* Tomb_Raider_4_Font (TR4 default)
* Tomb Raider (Author: "Navin_Ad" , resembles Tomb Raider (2013) font : https://www.dafont.com/tomb-raider2.font )
* TR (Author unknown. Resembles Tomb Raider (2001) / Tomb Raider: The Cradle of Light (2003) font https://www.dafont.com/tomb-raider.font)
* Tomb_Raider_AOD_Font (Author unknown , resembles Tomb Raider: The Angel of Darkness menu font)
